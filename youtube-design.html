Youtube UX/UI Redesign

Redesigned With a Minimal
and Modern Aesthetic

The design uses depth as not only a way to show contrast,
but also as a means of giving the viewer more comfort when viewing.
The layout remains familiar yet removes much of the bloat
modern Youtube has gained.

Reimagined Play Bar

The playbar is a tool, something used to accomplish a task. To ensure that it never stands
in the way of that task it is essential that the playbar contrasts well against the currently playing video.
By adding depth and shadows alongside blur, it amplifies the contrast viewed ensuring you will always be able
to see the controls.

Putting the "You"
Back In Youtube

Youtube through its many identity shifts through time has lost what made it special,
while this may be a more underlying issue, I believe user centric design goes a long way in making
the experience feel more personal and not just simply personalized. Having your account shown
up front at all times re-affirms the fact it is YOUR experience.

Changing The Way
Recommendations Are Handled

Perhaps the largest change in the user experience of Youtube is how you are able to
sort recommendations. Many times it is difficult to find something to watch on the homepage, having extra
filters fixes this issue. Not only being able to sort by Topics & Categories it allows
you to choose what form that content is in the form of tabs. Each media format is seperated making
sure your feed isn't cluttered enabling you to find a video more effectively.
